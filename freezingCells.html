Freezing Cells

What you need

1. Media
2. Trypsin
3. Flasks
4. Hood
5. Pipettes and Pipettor
6 15 mL tube

Prep

Make Freezing media
add 9.5 mL of your media into a 15 mL tube
add 500 uL of DMSO into same tube

Procedure

1.Tripsinize normally until after incubation for three min
2. Do not take out trypsin, add 7 ml media to flask
3. take all out and put into 15mL conical
4. add 10-12 ml media in flask again
5. centrifuge conical for 6-10 min
6. dump out excess liquid (ONLY ONCE-to isolate pellet)
7. add 3 ml freezing media
8. flick to resuspend pellet
9. take tiny white tube and label with cell line and initials AND MICRO-RNA (if applicable)
10. Split half and half into tubes
11. immediately put on ice
