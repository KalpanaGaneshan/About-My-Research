Trypsinization

What you need

1. Media
2. Trypsin
3. Flasks
4. Hood
5. Pipettes and Pipettor

Prep

Spray gloves and workspace
Place media and trypsin in hot water bath to thaw

Procedure

1. Place all materials under hood
2. Open cap of flask
3. Open 10 mL pipette and screw into pipetter
4. Take out all media out of flask and put in discard beaker
5. Open trypsin bottle
6. Take out 5 mL pipette (same as above)
7. Pipette 3 mL of trypsin in flask if you are working with a T-75 Flask and 1.5 mL of trypsin if you are working with a T-25 Flask
8. Tap flask to spread trypsin
9. Pipette out trypsin into waste beaker
10. Pipette same amount of trypsin (as before) and put in flask
11. Place the flask in the incubator for three minutes
12. View cells under microscope to make sure that cells are moving and not attached.
13. Pipette out trypsin into waste beaker
14. Add 10-12 mL media into flask and place in incubator
