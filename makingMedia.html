Making Media with G418

What you need

1. Appropriate Media Bottle
2. 10% fetal bovine serum
3. 1% penicillin/ streptomycin
4. G418
6. Reservoir
7. Eppindorf
8. Weighing Boat
9. Weighing scale
10. Filter

Prep

Spray gloves and workspace Thaw all materials

Procedure

1. Pour the contents of the two bottles into the media
2.Calculate how much g418 is needed for particular concentration (0.6 at least)
3. Measure out using electronic scale in small plastic eppindorf
4. Pipette 1 ml of media into eppindorf
5. Tap and mix powder in with media
6. Pipette 10-12 ml media out into reservoir
7. Pipette 1 ml media into reservoir
8. Open the top of filter and leave it in the plastic casing
9. Take 10mL out of reservoir into conical
10. Screw on filter
11. Release media into bottle
12. Repeat until all media is in bottle
